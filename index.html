About Me

Hello! I'm Westyn Hilliard, a data scientist with a strong passion for solving complex problems through technology. With a Master's in Data Science, I have been able to work on numerous projects specializing in utilizing data to uncover insights and create impactful solutions. I recently graduated with a Master's degree in Data Science, where I honed my skills in machine learning, data visualization, and predictive analytics. Throughout my career, I’ve been fortunate to work in finance, where I have had the opportunity to use my Data Science knowledge to make analytical decisions to increase operations while increasing business. What excites me the most about data science is its ability to turn raw data into meaningful stories that influence real-world outcomes. My approach is grounded in both creativity and precision; I strive to balance innovative thinking with an organized mindset to deliver accurate and actionable insights. Outside of my professional life, I’m passionate about continued learning and applying it in practical, real-world applications. Whether it’s staying up-to-date with the latest developments or refining my programming skills, I believe in the power of lifelong learning to stay ahead in the ever-evolving tech landscape. When I’m not diving deep into data, you can find me enjoying the outdoors of East Tennessee, playing pickleball, or spending time with my family. I’m always eager to connect with others, share knowledge, and collaborate on exciting new projects. If you’re interested in learning more about my work or discussing potential opportunities, feel free to reach out!
